Gta 5 Online

Mod Menus in GTA 5 Story mode / Online. Usually works with the latest patch.

Step 1

The first thing you need to do to begin modding GTA 5 is make sure that you are currently on the compatible platform. Cheating on consoles is impossible and can't be done. If there is a service that offers cheats for the console, it is most likely a scam. Cheating and modding is only available for PC copies of the game.

To begin, you must find out exactly what kind of mod menu you want and what your budget is. There are no good mod menus that are available for free. There are ofcourse free mod menus however they will get you banned from the online service of the game. Mod menus that are paid are tested and safe to use if you follow the developers advice. You shouldn't abuse them if you dont want to get banned.

Ultimately, mod menus range from £15 to £120. The most expensive mod menu is called 2take1, most mod menus offer the same services. However, some are not tested and stable as others. 2Take1 promises to be the best mod menu money can buy, it is tested, light and stable when it is loaded. It is also very quick to authenticate the user and load. The cheapest and safest mod menu you can get is called 'Disturbed', it is a tested mod menu that includes most features are found in the more expensive mod menus such as recoveries, god mode and spawning all vehicles in the game. There is fantastic mod menu called 'impulse one' which is very popular across the modding community. It is around £50 and it is definitely one of the best mod menus on the market.

Step 2

This step will explain how to purchase these mod menus. To purchase the 'Disturbed' mod menu, you must purchase it through the official website, there are 2 versions of the mod menu which you can purchase, the VIP version and the normal version. The VIP version contains more menu options which are available to modders. The link to the website is here. To pickup an impulse one license you need to get it through a reseller which is available on this website.

When you purchase the mod menu, you will be given an option to download the executable which will start the mod menu or an injector with a DLL file. The mod menus for GTA 5 work by attaching a DLL to the process. This overrides some of the functions that are exucted within the memory. Which enables you to execute the functions that are used by the mod menu.
